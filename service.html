What is Bye-Bye Bottles?

Bye-Bye Bottles is an app to educate people about Bottle Bills and motivate them to recycle containers, as well as a helper to locate bottle redemption centers near them. It's an app that people hopefully wouldn't need to use much for Bottle Bill information, and moreso an easy way to locate redemption centers near them.

Features of the app:

  • Login — so users can save their information or stay as a guest
  • Money tracker + camera access to scan barcodes — users can scan the barcode of a bottle before they recycle it and the app will track how much money they've made
  • Map — an in-app map will make it easy for users to locate a redemption center near them
  • Bottle Bill information — while the app will provide links to official Bottle Bill websites, it'll also provide an overview of Bottle Bills
  • Menu — for organization of app features and easy accessibility for the user

Web-app operations:

1. Users land on the site and log in or stay as a guest.
2. Users can click to the home page to get a brief overview of Bottle Bills.
3. Users can click the menu and go look at resources, the map, open the camera for scanning, or look at their money tracker.
4. In the map, users can input their ZIP code or county, or allow location services, to find their nearest redemption center within a certain radius that they can set.
5. From the camera section, they can scan a container zipcode and save its value to their money tracker.
6. In the money tracker, users can see how much money they've earned by recycling containers, and see a breakdown of what types of containers they've recycled.
