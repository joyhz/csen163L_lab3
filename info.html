About Bye-Bye Bottles

What are Bottle Bills?

Have you ever seen a small price amount on single-use bottles and cans? That small 5, 10, or maybe even 24 cent value you see is a result of Bottle Bills. Currently in place only in ten states in the US (but not worldwide), Bottle Bills place a small, refundable deposit on beverage containers. Upon returning an empty container to a redemption center, you can receive a few cents back! If you live in a Bottle Bill state but don't know where redemption centers are, Bye-Bye Bottles is the place to look!

Who is Bye-Bye Bottles for?

People of all ages in the U.S. can use Bye-Bye Bottles whether it's to find a redemption center near them, or learn more about Bottle Bills in the U.S. Even tourists visiting the U.S. can get a refundable deposit on bottles they bought in a Bottle Bill state!
Note: Bye-Bye Bottles is best for those who understand English.
